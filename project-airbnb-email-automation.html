Workflow Automation  2026

Airbnb Owner Statement
Email Automation

A property management company was manually emailing monthly financial statements to over 300 Airbnb property owners — one by one, every single month. The process consumed nearly three weeks of staff time and was highly prone to human error: wrong attachments, missed owners, inconsistent email content. I built a Google Sheets + Apps Script automation that reduced the entire workflow to a single trigger — cutting a 3-week manual process down to 2–5 days.

The Problem

Every month, the client's team had to open each owner's statement file, compose a personalized email, attach the correct PDF, and send it manually — repeated 300+ times. With no standardized template and everything done by hand, errors were inevitable: statements sent to the wrong owner, mismatched attachments, inconsistent messaging, and hours of repetitive data entry. The sheer volume meant staff spent roughly three weeks on a task that generated zero additional revenue. They needed a reliable, scalable solution that didn't require technical expertise to operate.

What I Did

01
Google Sheets Owner Database

Designed a structured Google Sheet as the central hub — capturing each owner's full name, property name, email address, and the corresponding statement file path. All data in one place, easy to update monthly.

02
Dynamic Email Template

Built a professional email template that pulls the owner's name and property details directly from the sheet — so every email reads as personally written, with the correct statement summary auto-populated in the body.

03
Automated File Attachment

Configured the script to automatically locate and attach each owner's specific statement file from Google Drive — eliminating the single biggest source of error: wrong attachments sent to the wrong recipient.

04
One-Click Dropdown Trigger

Added a simple dropdown in Column H of the sheet. When staff selects "Send" for any row, Apps Script fires immediately — sending the personalized email with the correct attachment. No coding knowledge required to operate.

05
Send Status Logging

The sheet automatically updates each row with a timestamp and confirmation status after every send — giving the team a clear audit trail of who was contacted and when, with zero manual tracking needed.

Tools Used

Google Sheets Google Apps Script Gmail API Google Drive

Outcome

300+ Owners Automated
~85% Time Reduction
3 weeks → 5 days Monthly Workflow
