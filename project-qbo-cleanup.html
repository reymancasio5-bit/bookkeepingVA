Catch-Up Bookkeeping  2025

12-Month QuickBooks Online Cleanup

An e-commerce business owner came to me with a full year of messy, uncategorized transactions and unreconciled accounts. Their books were unusable for tax preparation and gave them zero visibility into their actual financial health. I rebuilt everything from scratch in QuickBooks Online — and delivered clean, accurate books within two weeks.

The Problem

The client had been manually entering transactions without a proper Chart of Accounts structure, causing expenses to be miscategorized and income to be understated. Bank accounts had never been reconciled — meaning no one could confirm whether the books matched reality. With tax season approaching, they were stuck.

What I Did

01
Chart of Accounts Rebuild

Redesigned the entire Chart of Accounts to match the business structure — proper income, expense, asset, and liability categories.

02
Transaction Recategorization

Reviewed and recategorized every transaction across 12 months — over 400 entries — ensuring each one landed in the correct account.

03
Full Bank Reconciliation

Reconciled all bank and credit card accounts month by month, resolving every discrepancy until books matched bank statements 100%.

04
Financial Reports Delivered

Generated a clean Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet for the full year — ready for their accountant and tax filing.
